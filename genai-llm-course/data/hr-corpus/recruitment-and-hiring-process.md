# Recruitment and Hiring Process

**Acme Corp** | Effective January 1, 2026

## Overview

Acme Corp's talent acquisition team partners with hiring managers to fill roles efficiently and fairly. All open roles are posted on the internal job board (jobs.acmecorp.internal) and on external job boards simultaneously.

## Interview Loop Stages

Every role at Acme follows a standardized interview process to ensure consistency and reduce bias:

| Stage | Format | Owner | Typical Duration |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1. Recruiter Screen | 30-min video/phone | Talent Acquisition | Day 1–5 |
| 2. Hiring Manager Interview | 45-min video | Hiring Manager | Day 5–10 |
| 3. Technical / Skills Assessment | Take-home or live exercise | Panel | Day 7–14 |
| 4. Panel Interviews (×2–3) | 45-min each, structured | Cross-functional | Day 10–18 |
| 5. Values & Culture Interview | 30-min video | People Operations | Day 15–20 |
| 6. Reference Checks | 2–3 references | Talent Acquisition | Day 18–22 |
| 7. Offer | Written offer letter | Talent Acquisition | Day 20–25 |

Not all roles require all stages; the Talent Acquisition partner will specify the loop for each role.

## Offer Process

- Offers are extended verbally first, then followed by a written offer letter within 24 hours.
- Offers are contingent on a background check (criminal, employment verification, education verification where applicable).
- Candidates have **5 business days** to accept or decline an offer unless otherwise negotiated.

## Employee Referral Program

Acme Corp encourages employee referrals. If you refer a candidate who is hired and completes **90 days of employment**, you receive a **$2,000 referral bonus** (paid in the next payroll cycle after the 90-day mark).

- Submit referrals through Workday → **Referrals** before the candidate applies.
- Referrals submitted after a candidate has already applied are not eligible.
- People managers who directly influence hiring decisions for a role are not eligible for a referral bonus for that specific role.

## Internal Mobility

Current employees may apply for open roles after completing **6 months in their current role**. Notify your current manager before accepting an internal offer. Internal applicants go through the same interview loop.

## Interview Best Practices

- All interviewers must complete Acme's structured interview training (available in Workday) before conducting interviews.
- Use the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result) for behavioral questions.
- Submit interview feedback in the ATS (Greenhouse) within **24 hours** of the interview.
