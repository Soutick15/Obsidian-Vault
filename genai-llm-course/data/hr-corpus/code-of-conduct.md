# Code of Conduct

**Acme Corp** | Effective January 1, 2026

## Purpose

Acme Corp is committed to providing a respectful, inclusive, and safe environment for all employees, contractors, and partners — in the office, remote, and at company events.

## Expected Behaviors

All Acme Corp employees are expected to:

- Treat colleagues, customers, and partners with professionalism and respect.
- Communicate constructively and assume positive intent.
- Honor commitments and be transparent about blockers.
- Respect confidentiality of proprietary and personal information.
- Report concerns, mistakes, or misconduct promptly and honestly.

## Prohibited Conduct

The following are strictly prohibited and may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination:

- **Harassment**: Any verbal, written, or physical conduct that creates a hostile or intimidating environment, including sexual harassment, bullying, or threats.
- **Discrimination**: Treating any person adversely based on a protected characteristic (race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, disability, sexual orientation, gender identity, or veteran status).
- **Retaliation**: Taking adverse action against an employee for reporting a concern in good faith.
- **Dishonesty**: Falsifying records, misrepresenting information to colleagues, customers, or regulators.
- **Conflicts of Interest**: Failing to disclose relationships or interests that could influence business decisions.
- **Unauthorized Disclosure**: Sharing confidential, proprietary, or customer data with unauthorized parties.

## Conflicts of Interest

Employees must disclose any potential conflict of interest (e.g., a financial interest in a vendor, a family member working at a competitor) to their manager and People Operations. Disclosure does not automatically constitute a violation — undisclosed conflicts do.

## Reporting

If you witness or experience a violation of this Code:

1. **Speak with your manager** if you feel comfortable.
2. **Contact People Operations** directly at people-ops@acmecorp.internal.
3. **Use the anonymous Ethics Hotline**: ethics-hotline@acmecorp.internal or 1-800-555-0199.

All reports are taken seriously. Acme Corp prohibits retaliation against anyone who reports a concern in good faith.

## Investigations

People Operations will investigate all credible reports promptly and confidentially. Investigation timelines depend on complexity; employees can expect an initial acknowledgment within 2 business days.

---
*Violations may be reported anonymously. All Acme Corp employees are expected to complete the annual Code of Conduct training in Workday.*
