# Expense and Reimbursement Policy

**Acme Corp** | Effective January 1, 2026

## Overview

Acme Corp reimburses employees for reasonable, pre-approved business expenses. All expenses must be submitted within **30 days** of the expenditure via Workday. Late submissions may be denied.

## Spending Limits (No Pre-Approval Required)

| Category | Per-Transaction Limit |
|---|---|
| Meals (solo) | $30 |
| Meals (team / client) | $60 per person |
| Ground transportation (taxi, rideshare) | $75 per trip |
| Office supplies | $50 per item |
| Books / online courses | $100 |

Expenses above these limits require manager pre-approval before purchase.

## Travel

- **Flights**: Book at least 14 days in advance when possible. Economy/coach class for flights under 6 hours; business class permitted for flights 6+ hours with VP approval.
- **Hotels**: Up to **$250/night** in major cities, $175/night elsewhere. Use the corporate booking portal (TravelDesk) for best rates.
- **Car rental**: Compact or mid-size only. Always decline the rental agency's insurance — Acme's corporate policy covers business rentals.
- **Mileage reimbursement**: Personal vehicle use is reimbursed at the current IRS standard mileage rate (check the HR portal for the current rate).

## Meals and Entertainment

- Meals with external clients or candidates require a business justification and the names of attendees in the expense note.
- Alcohol is reimbursable only when included in a meal with a client or at a company-sanctioned event. A standalone bar tab is not reimbursable.

## Non-Reimbursable Expenses

The following are never reimbursable:
- Personal entertainment (movies, sporting events not related to business)
- Traffic tickets or parking violations
- Upgrades (seat upgrades, early boarding) paid personally
- Expenses without a receipt (amounts over $25 require a receipt)
- Expenses incurred by a spouse or family member unless they are an Acme employee on the same trip

## Submission Process

1. Log into Workday → **Expenses → Create Expense Report**.
2. Attach digital receipts for all items over $25.
3. Select the appropriate expense category and add a brief business justification.
4. Submit to your direct manager for approval. Finance reviews and processes approved reports within **5 business days**.
