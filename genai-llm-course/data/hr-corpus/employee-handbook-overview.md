# Employee Handbook Overview

**Acme Corp** — Internal Employee Handbook (Effective January 1, 2026)

## Welcome to Acme Corp

Acme Corp is a mid-sized technology company headquartered in Austin, Texas, with remote employees across the United States and Canada. We build enterprise workflow automation software for operations and finance teams.

## About This Handbook

This handbook summarizes Acme Corp's policies, benefits, and expectations. It is not a contract of employment. Full policy documents are maintained on the internal HR portal. When in doubt, contact People Operations at people-ops@acmecorp.internal.

## Our Values

- **Build Together** — Collaboration over heroics.
- **Own the Outcome** — Take accountability for your work.
- **Iterate Fast** — Ship, learn, improve.
- **Be Inclusive** — Every voice matters.

## Employment Basics

- All employees are at-will unless otherwise stated in a written employment agreement.
- Acme Corp is an equal opportunity employer and does not discriminate on the basis of race, color, national origin, sex, gender identity, age, disability, veteran status, or any other protected characteristic.
- Employment eligibility (I-9 verification) must be completed within three business days of your start date.

## Policy Updates

Acme Corp reserves the right to update policies at any time. Employees will be notified of material changes via company-wide email. The most current version of each policy lives on the HR portal.

## Acknowledgment

All employees are required to acknowledge receipt of this handbook during onboarding and whenever a significant policy update is issued.
