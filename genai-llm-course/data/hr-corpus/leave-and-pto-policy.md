# Leave and PTO Policy

**Acme Corp** | Effective January 1, 2026

## Vacation / PTO

Acme Corp uses an **accrual-based PTO model**:

| Years of Service | Annual PTO Accrual |
|---|---|
| 0–2 years | 15 days (120 hours) |
| 3–5 years | 20 days (160 hours) |
| 6+ years | 25 days (200 hours) |

PTO accrues each pay period (bi-weekly). New employees begin accruing from day one and may use accrued PTO immediately — there is no waiting period.

**Carryover**: Up to 5 unused PTO days may carry over into the next calendar year. Any balance above 5 days is forfeited on January 1.

## How to Request PTO

1. Submit a request in the HR portal (Workday) at least **5 business days** in advance for absences of 3+ days; shorter notice is acceptable for urgent personal matters.
2. Obtain manager approval before confirming plans.
3. For extended absences (10+ business days), notify your team at least 3 weeks ahead.

## Sick Leave

Employees accrue **1 hour of sick leave per 30 hours worked**, up to **10 days (80 hours) per year**. Sick leave does not carry over and is separate from PTO. It may be used for personal illness, medical appointments, or to care for an immediate family member.

## Parental Leave

- **Birth parent / primary caregiver**: 16 weeks of fully paid parental leave.
- **Secondary caregiver / non-birth parent**: 6 weeks of fully paid parental leave.
- Leave must be taken within 12 months of the child's birth, adoption, or placement.
- Employees must have been employed for at least 6 months to be eligible.
- To initiate parental leave, notify People Operations at least 30 days in advance (or as soon as practicable).

## Bereavement Leave

Up to **5 paid days** for the death of an immediate family member (spouse, child, parent, sibling). Up to **2 paid days** for extended family (grandparent, in-law, aunt/uncle).

## Jury Duty

Employees called for jury duty receive full pay for up to 10 business days. Provide your summons notice to People Operations promptly.
