# Compensation and Promotion

**Acme Corp** | Effective January 1, 2026

## Compensation Philosophy

Acme Corp targets **market-competitive compensation** at the 65th percentile of the relevant talent market (software/tech industry, US/Canada). We use Radford/Aon survey data, refreshed annually, to benchmark roles.

Total compensation includes base salary, annual merit increase, equity grants, and benefits.

## Salary Bands

Roles are grouped into **job levels** (L1–L7). Each level has a salary band:

| Level | Title Examples | Band Range (USD/year) |
|---|---|---|
| L1 | Associate Engineer, Coordinator | $65,000 – $85,000 |
| L2 | Engineer I, Analyst | $80,000 – $105,000 |
| L3 | Engineer II, Senior Analyst | $100,000 – $135,000 |
| L4 | Senior Engineer, Lead Analyst | $130,000 – $175,000 |
| L5 | Staff Engineer, Manager | $165,000 – $220,000 |
| L6 | Principal Engineer, Senior Manager | $210,000 – $280,000 |
| L7 | Distinguished Engineer, Director+ | $270,000+ |

Band ranges are reviewed and updated each January. Employees whose pay falls below the band floor are brought up at the next merit cycle.

## Annual Merit Increases

Merit increases are effective **March 1** each year, based on the prior year's Annual Review rating:

| Rating | Typical Merit Increase Range |
|---|---|
| 5 — Exceptional | 8–12% |
| 4 — Exceeds Expectations | 5–8% |
| 3 — Meets Expectations | 3–5% |
| 2 — Partially Meets | 0% |
| 1 — Does Not Meet | 0% (PIP required) |

Increases are subject to overall budget and cannot push an employee above the band maximum without a promotion.

## Promotions

Promotions to the next level require:

1. **Sustained performance** at the next-level standard, typically demonstrated over at least one review cycle.
2. **Manager nomination** submitted during the Annual Review cycle.
3. **Calibration approval** by the department head and People Operations.
4. Promotions are effective **March 1** alongside merit increases.

Off-cycle promotions are rare and require VP approval.

## Equity

- Equity is granted as **Restricted Stock Units (RSUs)**.
- Standard new-hire grants vest over **4 years with a 1-year cliff** (25% at month 12, then monthly thereafter).
- Refresh grants are available to high performers and are reviewed annually.
- Equity grant values and vesting schedules are detailed in the individual grant agreement in Carta.

## Pay Transparency

Salary bands are shared openly with all employees. Employees may discuss their compensation with colleagues; Acme Corp does not prohibit pay discussions.
