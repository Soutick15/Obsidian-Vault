# Benefits and Insurance

**Acme Corp** | Effective January 1, 2026

Benefits become effective on the **first day of the month following your start date**. Open enrollment runs each November for the following plan year.

## Health Insurance

Acme Corp offers three tiers through MedFirst Health Network:

| Plan | Employee Premium (monthly) | Acme Contribution |
|---|---|---|
| Bronze HDHP | $0 | 100% of premium |
| Silver PPO | $85 | ~80% of premium |
| Gold PPO | $210 | ~70% of premium |

Dependents may be added to any plan. The company contributes an additional 60% of dependent premiums.

**Health Savings Account (HSA)**: Employees enrolled in the Bronze HDHP receive an annual Acme HSA contribution of **$750 (individual) or $1,500 (family)**.

## Dental Insurance

Acme Corp covers **100% of the employee dental premium** under the SmileFirst PPO plan. Dependent dental coverage is available at $45/month for one dependent, $90/month for family. The plan includes 2 annual cleanings, X-rays, and covers 80% of basic restorative procedures.

## Vision Insurance

Basic vision coverage (one exam + $150 frame/lens allowance per year) is provided at no cost to the employee. Optional upgraded coverage is available at $12/month.

## Retirement — 401(k)

- Acme Corp offers a traditional and Roth 401(k) through Fidelity.
- **Company match**: 100% of employee contributions up to **4% of gross salary**.
- Vesting: Company match vests on a **3-year graded schedule** (34%/67%/100%).
- Employees are eligible to enroll immediately upon hire.

## Life and Disability Insurance

- **Basic Life Insurance**: 2× annual salary, employer-paid.
- **Short-Term Disability (STD)**: 60% of salary for up to 12 weeks, employer-paid.
- **Long-Term Disability (LTD)**: 60% of salary after STD period, employer-paid.

## Wellness Benefit

Acme Corp reimburses up to **$600 per year** for wellness expenses: gym memberships, fitness apps, ergonomic home-office equipment, or mental-health app subscriptions. Submit receipts in Workday under "Wellness Reimbursement."

## Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

All employees and household members have free, confidential access to 6 counseling sessions per issue per year through Acme's EAP partner, CareConnect.
