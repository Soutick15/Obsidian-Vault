# Company Holidays 2026

**Acme Corp** | Calendar Year 2026

Acme Corp observes the following **12 paid company holidays** in 2026. All US and Canada employees receive these days off with full pay. Canadian employees also receive any additional provincially mandated statutory holidays for their province (contact people-ops for your province-specific calendar).

## Holiday Falling on Weekend

- If a holiday falls on a **Saturday**, the preceding Friday is observed.
- If a holiday falls on a **Sunday**, the following Monday is observed.

Independence Day (July 4) falls on a Saturday in 2026; the observed day off for US employees is **Friday, July 3, 2026**.

## Floating Holiday

In addition to the scheduled holidays above, each employee receives **1 floating holiday** per calendar year to use at their discretion. Submit it in Workday the same way as PTO. Floating holidays do not carry over to the next year.

## Holiday Pay for Part-Time Employees

Part-time employees receive holiday pay proportional to their scheduled hours on that day of the week.

## Working on a Holiday

Non-exempt (hourly) employees who are required to work on a company holiday receive 1.5× their regular hourly rate for hours worked, plus their holiday pay — consult payroll@acmecorp.internal for details.
