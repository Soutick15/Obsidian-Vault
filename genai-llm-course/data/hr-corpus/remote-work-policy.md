# Remote Work Policy

**Acme Corp** | Effective January 1, 2026

## Overview

Acme Corp is a **remote-first company**. The majority of roles are fully remote. Some roles in hardware, lab operations, and executive leadership may require on-site presence; this will be stated explicitly in the job description and offer letter.

## Eligibility

All regular full-time and part-time employees in approved remote-eligible roles may work remotely. Contract workers and interns follow the terms stated in their agreements.

## Work Location Requirements

- Employees must work from a location within the **United States or Canada** unless a formal international remote arrangement has been approved by Legal and People Operations.
- International remote work requests must be submitted at least 60 days in advance. Approval is not guaranteed and depends on tax and employment-law considerations.
- Employees must maintain a dedicated, reasonably private workspace with reliable internet (minimum 25 Mbps download / 10 Mbps upload).

## Core Hours

To support collaboration across time zones, all employees are expected to be available during **core hours of 10 AM – 3 PM Central Time**, Monday through Friday, unless on approved leave.

Outside core hours, employees have flexibility to structure their day as needed to meet their deliverables.

## Home Office Stipend

New employees receive a **one-time home-office setup stipend of $750** to cover desk, chair, monitors, or other equipment. This is paid as a reimbursement — submit receipts within 90 days of your start date.

Employees may also request standard IT equipment (laptop, keyboard, webcam) from the IT department at no cost.

## In-Person Events

Acme Corp hosts **two all-hands gatherings per year** (typically March and September). Attendance is strongly encouraged. Travel and lodging are covered by the company. Teams may also organize optional off-sites; budget approval is required from the department head.

## Security While Remote

Employees must follow the IT and Security Policy at all times, including use of the VPN for accessing internal systems, locking screens when away from the workstation, and never working on sensitive data in public spaces without a privacy screen.

## Coworking Spaces

Acme reimburses up to **$150/month** for a coworking space membership, submitted via Workday under "Coworking Reimbursement."
