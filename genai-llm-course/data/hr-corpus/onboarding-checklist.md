# Onboarding Checklist

**Acme Corp** | New Employee Onboarding Guide

Welcome to Acme Corp! This checklist covers what to expect and complete during your first month. Your onboarding buddy and manager will help guide you through each phase.

---

## Before Day One (Completed by HR / IT)

- [ ] Offer letter signed and countersigned
- [ ] Background check cleared
- [ ] Workday account created and welcome email sent
- [ ] Laptop shipped or ready for pickup
- [ ] Slack, email (Google Workspace), and core app accounts provisioned
- [ ] Welcome package (swag) shipped

---

## Week 1 — First Days

- [ ] Attend company-wide onboarding session (virtual, every Monday at 10 AM CT)
- [ ] Meet with your manager: align on role expectations and 30/60/90-day goals
- [ ] Complete I-9 verification (must be done within 3 business days)
- [ ] Set up your laptop following the IT Security Setup Guide (in Workday → Documents)
- [ ] Enroll in benefits (you have 30 days from your start date)
- [ ] Complete compliance trainings: Code of Conduct, Data Privacy, Security Awareness (Workday → Learning)
- [ ] Set up 1:1s with your direct teammates
- [ ] Meet your onboarding buddy (assigned by People Operations)

---

## Week 2–3 — Getting Oriented

- [ ] Complete role-specific product training (ask your manager for the relevant module)
- [ ] Shadow at least 2 customer calls or internal team meetings
- [ ] Review the team's OKRs and current project roadmap
- [ ] Gain access to team tools (Jira, Confluence, GitHub/GitLab, etc. — submit requests via helpdesk@acmecorp.internal)
- [ ] Set up direct deposit in Workday (payroll@acmecorp.internal for questions)
- [ ] Expense your home office setup (submit receipts within 90 days)

---

## Month 1 — Ramping Up

- [ ] Complete your first 30-day check-in with your manager
- [ ] Document any onboarding gaps or missing access and report to helpdesk
- [ ] Complete the Employee Referral Program overview in Workday
- [ ] Submit any remaining benefit enrollment changes before the 30-day window closes
- [ ] Begin working on your first goal in Workday (aligned with manager)
- [ ] Confirm your emergency contact information is saved in Workday
